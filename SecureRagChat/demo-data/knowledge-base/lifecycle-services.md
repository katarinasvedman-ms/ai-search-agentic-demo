# Lifecycle Services

## Public overview

Lifecycle Services packages onboarding, software maintenance, and operational readiness across radio, transport, and core environments. Public descriptions emphasize lower operational risk, faster modernization, and coordinated service outcomes.

## Protected service notes

Protected material covers incident escalation ownership, named support roles, executive notification thresholds, and service workflows tied to different entitlement levels. These details are intentionally separate from the public service overview.

## Operational role

Lifecycle Services connects the technology portfolio to a support and change-management story. It is especially useful in demos where the user asks what happens after deployment or how incidents are handled.

## Demo intent

Use this source when you want the agentic path to combine product architecture with operational support context rather than just hardware or topology detail.
