# WaveSync 8600

## Public overview

WaveSync 8600 is a transport and timing platform for radio aggregation, fronthaul resiliency, and synchronization assurance. It is positioned for operators that need weatherized deployment options and tighter control over timing-sensitive radio domains.

## Protected engineering notes

Protected documentation includes optics compatibility, holdover alarm guidance, mixed-shelf restrictions, and corrective actions for timing drift events. Long-reach 25G optics require release 24.3 or later, and mixed optics deployments should enable holdover alarms before site activation.

## Operational role

WaveSync is typically paired with Aurora macro radios for outdoor site clusters and with private network rollouts that need stable fronthaul and aggregation. It becomes especially important where multiple radio families depend on shared timing.

## Related products

Aurora RAN 6651 relies on WaveSync 8600 for transport and timing support in many macro deployments. Manufacturing blueprints combine WaveSync with Aurora, Nimbus, and CoreSphere to present a complete private 5G architecture.
