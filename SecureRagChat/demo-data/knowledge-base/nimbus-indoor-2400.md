# Nimbus Indoor 2400

## Public overview

Nimbus Indoor 2400 is a compact 5G indoor small cell platform for offices, hospitals, stadium concourses, and transport hubs. The product focuses on floor-by-floor coverage shaping and centralized management of dense indoor domains.

## Protected engineering notes

Nimbus requires PoE++ power and supports shared management for up to 96 indoor cells per controller domain. Protected reference material includes cabling limits, antenna patterns, venue zoning templates, and controller dependency notes for high-density deployments.

## Planning guidance

Nimbus is recommended when users need indoor mobility, wall or ceiling mounting, or precise zone control in venues where outdoor macro coverage alone is insufficient. It complements, rather than replaces, outdoor macro radios such as Aurora RAN 6651.

## Related products

Private 5G manufacturing blueprints often combine Aurora outdoor coverage at the site perimeter with Nimbus cells inside production halls and logistics corridors.
