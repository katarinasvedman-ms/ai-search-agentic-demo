# Aurora RAN 6651

## Public overview

Aurora RAN 6651 is a mid-band macro radio intended for dense urban and suburban 5G coverage. It is positioned for operators that need higher sector throughput without expanding the physical footprint of existing rooftop or pole sites.

## Protected deployment notes

The protected deployment package assumes 4T4R operation, dual fiber fronthaul uplinks, and integrated GPS timing. Field teams must complete grounding, optics validation, and timing calibration before sector activation. The acceptance target is below 1.5 microseconds timing offset after fronthaul lock.

## Operational considerations

Aurora is the preferred product for outdoor macro layers, wide-area capacity expansion, and rural-to-suburban overlay refreshes. It is not intended to replace indoor small cells where venue zoning and floor-level mobility tuning are required.

## Related products

WaveSync 8600 is the preferred fronthaul and transport companion. Nimbus Indoor 2400 is used when the design objective shifts from outdoor sector coverage to indoor mobility or venue densification.
